Fun Puns!!!

By Adam Murphy

homepage Here are our top 10 puns.

1. I wondered why the baseball was getting bigger, then it hit me.

2. A man just attacked me with cream, milk and butter, how dairy.

3. I was reading a book about anti-gravity, it was impossible to put down.

4. Did you hear about the guy who's whole left side was cut off? He is alright now.

5. I used to be a banker, but I lost interest.

6. I don't trust these stairs because they are always up to something.

7. Someone assaulted me with a cup of tea and was charged for mugging.

8. I really wanted a camouflage shirt, but I couldn't find one.

9. Did you hear about the guy who got hit in the head with a can of soda? He was lucky it was a soft drink.

10. My friend's bakery burned down last night. Now his business is toast.
